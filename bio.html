Kai Luen is a Chinese-American sound artist, music producer/ composer currently based between Beijing and Los Angeles.

His music has been released by SBKVLT, Ran Music, BBE records, and Modern Sky. He is part of Beijing experimental music/ art group Wan Who? with Zhao Xia Xia and Mr. Asbo. He also has produced and released music as [Soulspeak] and [Silicone Moons].

I am a creator of sound, an observer of sound.

An person interested in the intersections/ inter.inner.connections/ of sound, visual, diy tinkering, ghost notes, and silence.



  • ..audio and visual feedback systems
  • .......taking simple objects and multiplying them
  • ..............investigating different processes for resonance and sound creation
  • ...................an exploration of audio visual relationships in real time graphics

Kai-Luen/ Soulspeak/ Silicone Moons.
