Kai Luen Kai Luen Liang is a LA based multidisciplinary artist working with generative systems, found image and sound objects, low tech/ DIY electronics, and open source digital tools. He is interested in ideas of appropriation/ sampling, culture clashes, and noise in generative sound and image making.

interested in the intersections/ inter.inner.connections/ of sound, visual, diy tinkering, ghost notes, and silence.
